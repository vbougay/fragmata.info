# Fragmata Community Posts

## Formatting guide

Each post needs two copy-paste code blocks ‚Äî one for each platform:

**WhatsApp** ‚Äî supports `*bold*` natively when pasted. Wrap key numbers and headings in single asterisks.

**Telegram** ‚Äî does NOT parse markdown from pasted text (asterisks show literally). Use plain text without any `*` markers. To bold after pasting: select text and press Cmd+B.

**Both platforms:**
- Emojis work as-is
- Use `‚Ä¢` for bullet points
- Plain URLs auto-link (no markdown link syntax)
- End with a üîó link to `https://fragmata.info`

**Post types:**
- Minor data updates: start with üìä, include total storage %, key highlights, dashboard link
- Big updates (with article): start with üì∞, include summary, bullet points, article link + dashboard link

# Welcome to the Fragmata community

This is the official community for [Fragmata](https://fragmata.info) ‚Äî a visual dashboard tracking water levels across all 21 reservoirs in Cyprus.

## What is Fragmata?

Cyprus has been experiencing a severe drought, and water reserves are a topic that affects everyone on the island. Fragmata brings together dam data from the Water Development Department ‚Äî going back to 1988 ‚Äî and turns it into clear visuals, maps, and forecasts so you can see exactly where things stand.

## What to expect here

- Updates when new reservoir data is published
- New feature announcements
- Drought forecasts and analysis
- Discussion about Cyprus water situation

## Explore

- Dashboard: [fragmata.info](https://fragmata.info)
- Individual dam pages for all 21 reservoirs
- Interactive map, historical charts, and drain date forecasts
- Available in English, Greek, and Russian

Feel free to share your thoughts, ask questions, or suggest features. This community is here for anyone who cares about Cyprus and its water future.

## March 18 ‚Äî Is the Drought Retreating?

New analysis published on Fragmata: **"Is the Drought Retreating? 12 of 21 Cyprus Reservoirs Exceed Last Year's Levels"**

Total storage at **22.2%** (64.7 MCM). The sustained Troodos rainfall keeps pushing numbers up, and the picture is changing fast:

- **12 of 21 reservoirs now exceed last year's levels**
- Xyliatos surged to 80.1% ‚Äî up from 66.2% just 9 days ago, 3.5x last year
- Evretou at 28.7%, new annual max
- Season inflow at 45.8 MCM, well past last year's full total
- Kouris at 20.4%, nearly matching last year
- WDD is optimistic: saturated ground is helping runoff

The forecast model we introduced in our [March 2 article](https://fragmata.info/articles/2026-03-02-cyprus-reservoirs-forecast) had an optimistic "recovery scenario" ‚Äî and that's now the trajectory we're actually on.

Southeast lowlands (Achna, Kalavasos, Lefkara) remain a concern ‚Äî but overall the trend is clearly positive.

Full article: [fragmata.info/articles/2026-03-18-drought-retreating](https://fragmata.info/articles/2026-03-18-drought-retreating)

## March 23 ‚Äî Reserves Exceed Last Year for the First Time

New analysis published: **"Cyprus Reservoirs Exceed Last Year for the First Time ‚Äî 12 mln. m¬≥ in 3 Days, Xyliatos Overflows"**

What a difference 3 days can make. An exceptional Troodos rainfall event delivered 12 mln. m¬≥ of inflow in just 72 hours, pushing total storage to **26.9%** (78.1 MCM) ‚Äî **surpassing last year's level (24.7%) for the first time this season**.

The numbers are dramatic:
- **Kouris at 24.3%** ‚Äî new season high, now above last year (20.1%)
- **Asprokremmos at 29.4%** ‚Äî surpassed last year's peak
- **Germasoyeia nearly doubled** in 3 days: 14.1% to 22.4%
- **Arminou surged to 56.6%** ‚Äî 2.46 MCM inflow in the 3-day event
- **Tamassos at 83.4%**, more than double last year
- **Xyliatos overflowed** ‚Äî exactly as our [March 18 article](https://fragmata.info/articles/2026-03-18-drought-retreating) predicted

Season total inflow: 59.8 MCM ‚Äî 3.2x the entire 2024/25 drought season. March alone has delivered 20.7 MCM.

The southeast (Achna at 2.1%, Kalavasos at 8.4%) still hasn't benefited, and the 10% water supply cut remains. But the overall trajectory has fundamentally shifted.

Full article: [fragmata.info/articles/2026-03-23-reserves-exceed-last-year](https://fragmata.info/articles/2026-03-23-reserves-exceed-last-year)
