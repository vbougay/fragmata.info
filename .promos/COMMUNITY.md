# Fragmata Community Posts

## Formatting guide

Each post needs two copy-paste code blocks тАФ one for each platform:

**WhatsApp** тАФ supports `*bold*` natively when pasted. Wrap key numbers and headings in single asterisks.

**Telegram** тАФ does NOT parse markdown from pasted text (asterisks show literally). Use plain text without any `*` markers. To bold after pasting: select text and press Cmd+B.

**Both platforms:**
- Emojis work as-is
- Use `тАв` for bullet points
- Plain URLs auto-link (no markdown link syntax)
- End with a ЁЯФЧ link to `https://fragmata.info`

**Post types:**
- Minor data updates: start with ЁЯУК, include total storage %, key highlights, dashboard link
- Big updates (with article): start with ЁЯУ░, include summary, bullet points, article link + dashboard link

# Welcome to the Fragmata community

This is the official community for [Fragmata](https://fragmata.info) тАФ a visual dashboard tracking water levels across all 21 reservoirs in Cyprus.

## What is Fragmata?

Cyprus has been experiencing a severe drought, and water reserves are a topic that affects everyone on the island. Fragmata brings together dam data from the Water Development Department тАФ going back to 1988 тАФ and turns it into clear visuals, maps, and forecasts so you can see exactly where things stand.

## What to expect here

- Updates when new reservoir data is published
- New feature announcements
- Drought forecasts and analysis
- Discussion about Cyprus water situation

## Explore

- Dashboard: [fragmata.info](https://fragmata.info)
- Individual dam pages for all 21 reservoirs
- Interactive map, historical charts, and drain date forecasts
- Available in English, Greek, and Russian

Feel free to share your thoughts, ask questions, or suggest features. This community is here for anyone who cares about Cyprus and its water future.

## March 18 тАФ Is the Drought Retreating?

New analysis published on Fragmata: **"Is the Drought Retreating? 12 of 21 Cyprus Reservoirs Exceed Last Year's Levels"**

Total storage at **22.2%** (64.7 MCM). The sustained Troodos rainfall keeps pushing numbers up, and the picture is changing fast:

- **12 of 21 reservoirs now exceed last year's levels**
- Xyliatos surged to 80.1% тАФ up from 66.2% just 9 days ago, 3.5x last year
- Evretou at 28.7%, new annual max
- Season inflow at 45.8 MCM, well past last year's full total
- Kouris at 20.4%, nearly matching last year

Southeast lowlands (Achna, Kalavasos, Lefkara) remain a concern тАФ but overall the trend is clearly positive.

## March 23 тАФ Reserves Exceed Last Year for the First Time

New analysis published: **"Cyprus Reservoirs Exceed Last Year for the First Time тАФ 12 mln. m┬│ in 3 Days, Xyliatos Overflows"**

The numbers are dramatic:
- **Kouris at 24.3%** тАФ new season high, now above last year (20.1%)
- **Asprokremmos at 29.4%** тАФ surpassed last year's peak
- **Germasoyeia nearly doubled** in 3 days: 14.1% to 22.4%
- **Arminou surged to 56.6%** тАФ 2.46 MCM inflow in the 3-day event
- **Tamassos at 83.4%**, more than double last year
- **Xyliatos overflowed** тАФ exactly as our [March 18 article](https://fragmata.info/articles/2026-03-18-drought-retreating) predicted

Season total inflow: 59.8 MCM тАФ 3.2x the entire 2024/25 drought season. March alone has delivered 20.7 MCM.

The southeast (Achna at 2.1%, Kalavasos at 8.4%) still hasn't benefited, and the 10% water supply cut remains. But the overall trajectory has fundamentally shifted.

Full article: [fragmata.info/articles/2026-03-23-reserves-exceed-last-year](https://fragmata.info/articles/2026-03-23-reserves-exceed-last-year)
